# Product Requirement Document (PRD) - Nuren Group Website

## 1. Project Overview
**Project Name:** Nuren Group Corporate Website
**Objective:** Establish a professional digital presence that showcases Nuren Group as a community-powered commerce platform for brands targeting families in Southeast Asia.

## 2. Target Audience
*   **Brands & Merchants:** FMCG, healthcare, and family lifestyle companies looking for growth.
*   **Partners/Influencers:** Individuals and organizations looking to join the ecosystem.
*   **Investors:** Seeking information on Nuren's regional impact and vision.

## 3. Key Features & Sections
1.  **Hero Section:** High-impact introduction to Nuren Group as a "Community-Powered Commerce Platform."
2.  **Ecosystem Showcase:** Interactive display of core products (Motherhood.com.my, Ibuencer, etc.).
3.  **Impact Metrics:** Data-driven section highlighting 5M+ users and 5,000+ merchants.
4.  **Brand Solutions:** Detailed breakdown of services (Sampling, Influencer strategies, Data targeting).
5.  **Vision & Positioning:** Clear articulation of Nuren's role as a growth engine in Southeast Asia.
6.  **Contact/Lead Gen:** Simple call-to-action for brands to partner.

## 4. Design Principles
*   **Trustworthy & Professional:** High-quality typography and clean layouts.
*   **Community-Centric:** Use of warm, engaging imagery and soft accents.
*   **Modern & Responsive:** Seamless experience across mobile and desktop.

## 5. Technical Requirements
*   **Framework:** React 19 with TypeScript.
*   **Styling:** Tailwind CSS 4.
*   **Animations:** Motion (Framer Motion).
*   **Icons:** Lucide-React.
